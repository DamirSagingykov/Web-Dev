Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti400g
Tomatoes6
Garlic2 cloves
Olive oil2 tbsp
Saltto taste

Steps:

  1. Boil water with salt
  2. Cook spaghetti for 10 minutes
  3. Chop tomatoes and garlic
  4. Cook tomatoes and garlic in olive oil
  5. Mix sauce with spaghetti

Green Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce1 head
Cucumber1
Tomato2
Olive oil3 tbsp
Lemon juice1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Wash lettuce and vegetables
  2. Chop lettuce, cucumber, and tomatoes
  3. Mix in a bowl
  4. Add olive oil and lemon juice
  5. Mix well and serve

Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour200g
Milk300ml
Eggs2
Baking powder2 tsp
Saltpinch

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt
  2. Add eggs and milk
  3. Mix until smooth
  4. Heat a pan with oil
  5. Cook pancakes until golden brown

Source: BBC Good Food
